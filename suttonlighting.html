LED replacement schemes

Most lighting in the borough was replaced during 2013-2017, on main roads using Teceo 1 and 2s and Axia 1s and later Amperas. Side roads were replaced with Axia 1s. These replaced mostly SOX but also SON and CPO. Afterwards replacements on side roads gradually continued with Axia 1s. However, there was still a decent amount of SOX surviving from the 1970s and 1980s. However, in 2021 VolkerHighways were appointed with a 10 year maintenance scheme. 2024 was the death year for most remaining non-LED lighting, with virtually all Sapphires replaced by May 2024 and the SOX rapidly been replaced as well, with Axia 3s and Axia 3 Evos. I will be visiting these in August 2024, and images and reports will arrive then.
